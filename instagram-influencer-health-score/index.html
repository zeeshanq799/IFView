What Is Influencer Health Score?

Influencer health score is a comprehensive metric (0-100) that quantifies creator value by combining multiple performance indicators into a single, actionable number.

Why brands use health scores:

  • Objective evaluation: Removes subjective assessment bias
  • Quick filtering: Screen hundreds of creators efficiently
  • Budget allocation: Match creator tiers to campaign budgets
  • ROI prediction: Score correlates with campaign performance

How to Improve Your Health Score

Strategic optimization based on weighted factors:

Priority 1: Increase Engagement (40% Weight)

  • Post consistently: 4-7 times weekly maintains visibility
  • Ask questions: Drive comment engagement
  • Create save-worthy content: Tutorials, tips, resources
  • Respond to comments: Within first hour boosts algorithmic ranking
  • Use CTAs: "Double-tap if you agree" proven to increase engagement

Priority 2: Maintain Growth (25% Weight)

  • Reels strategy: 3-5 Reels weekly for maximum reach
  • Collaboration: Cross-promote with similar-sized creators
  • Hashtag research: Mix of niche and trending tags
  • Post timing: Peak audience activity hours

Priority 3: Optimize Audience Quality (10% Weight)

  • Create English content: Attracts Tier 1 audiences
  • Use geo-tags: US/UK cities in posts
  • Remove fake followers: Periodic cleanup maintains quality
