What Brands Look For in 2026

Brand priorities have shifted dramatically from vanity metrics to performance indicators:

1. Engagement Rate > Follower Count

Brands now prioritize creators with 6%+ engagement over those with larger but less engaged audiences. A creator with 20K followers and 8% engagement is more valuable than one with 100K followers and 2% engagement.

Why: High engagement signals authentic audience connection and drives better conversion rates for brand products.

2. Audience Authenticity

Brands audit follower quality using tools like HypeAuditor and Social Blade. Fake followers, bots, or engagement pods disqualify creators from premium deals.

Red flags: Sudden follower spikes, low comment quality, engagement rate below 2%, or follower-to-view ratio discrepancies.

3. Content Quality and Production Value

Professional lighting, clear audio, and polished editing justify premium rates. Brands expect creators to deliver content that matches their brand standards without extensive revisions.

4. Niche Expertise

Subject matter experts command 30-50% premiums. A fitness creator with certifications charges more than a general lifestyle creator posting workout content.

5. Audience Demographics

US/UK audiences generate the highest brand value. Creators with 70%+ Tier 1 country audience can charge premium rates even with smaller follower counts.

How to Negotiate TikTok Brand Deals

Professional negotiation tactics that increase your earnings:

1. Know Your Metrics

Before any negotiation, have these numbers ready:

  • Engagement rate: Last 10 posts average
  • Average views per video: Demonstrate reach beyond followers
  • Audience demographics: Age, gender, location breakdown
  • Traffic sources: Organic vs paid vs profile views
  • Previous campaign performance: Conversion rates, click-throughs

2. Start High, Negotiate Down

Quote your ideal rate first. Brands expect to negotiate down 15-25%. If you want $1,000, quote $1,200-$1,300 to land at your target after negotiation.

3. Bundle Services for Higher Rates

Instead of single posts at $1,000 each, offer packages:

  • Standard Package: 1 post ($1,000)
  • Growth Package: 3 posts + story ($2,500 - 17% discount)
  • Premium Package: 5 posts + usage rights ($4,500 - 10% discount)

4. Charge Separately for Usage Rights

Never include usage rights in your base rate. Structure pricing as:

  • Post creation: $1,000
  • 30-day usage rights: +$300 (30%)
  • 90-day usage rights: +$500 (50%)
  • Perpetual rights: +$1,000 (100%)

5. Build Rate Card Tiers

Create a professional rate card with clear pricing:

Example Rate Card

Standard TikTok Post: $1,200

TikTok Story (24hr): $600

TikTok Live Mention: $1,500

UGC Video (no posting): $800

Usage Rights (90 days): +50%

Exclusivity (30 days): +30%

6. Ask for Performance Bonuses

Negotiate bonus payments for overperformance:

  • +20% if video exceeds 500K views
  • +10% if engagement rate exceeds 8%
  • +15% if drives X conversions (trackable links only)
